Cape Town

I have been dating my girlfriend for more than 3 years now, and last summer I had the slightly terrifying opportunity to meet her parents for the first time. Luckily, being from Cape Town, this meant a chance to visit the wonderful city. During my stay I had the chance to go whale watching, penguin watching, and go to the game reserves where I was luckily enough to see the ‘big 5’ up close and personal.
